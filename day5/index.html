ABOUT

BR Architects is a small architecture firm based in New York City. We specialize in residential projects, including new construction, renovations, and additions. Our work is characterized by clean lines, simple forms, and a focus on natural materials. We believe that good design can improve the quality of life for our clients, and we strive to create spaces that are both beautiful and functional. Our goal is to create architecture that is timeless, sustainable, and responsive to the needs of our clients.
